# About React UX4G

## What is UX4G?
**UX4G (User Experience for Government)** is the official design system developed by the National Informatics Centre (NIC) and the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) for the Government of India. Its primary goal is to bring uniformity, high accessibility, and a premium user experience to all government digital services.

React UX4G is the React implementation of this design system. It leverages the power of React to deliver interactive, stateful components while strictly adhering to the design principles, color palettes, typography, and accessibility guidelines laid out by the UX4G initiative.

## Our Mission
To simplify the UI development process for React developers building civic tech, e-governance platforms, and enterprise applications by providing a cohesive set of premium, ready-to-use components that are fully compliant with government standards.

## Core Principles
1. **Accessibility First:** We believe the web should be usable by everyone. Our components automatically manage critical ARIA roles and states.
2. **Beautiful Defaults:** Our components look great right out of the box, featuring modern design aesthetics like rounded corners, subtle shadows, and smooth transitions.
3. **Unobtrusive Abstractions:** We provide sensible defaults while exposing the necessary props to let you customize exactly what you need without fighting the library.
